Razer Mamba 2020 Review: Worth It?

Posted by Davindran on October 4, 2020

The Razer Mamba was first released in 2009 as Razer’s first wireless gaming mouse that pushed boundaries back then with it’s 4G laser that was more precise than any other wireless mouse in the market at that time. 11 years later, does it still stand as the king of wireless gaming mice?

Specifications

The Razer Mamba features a 5G advanced optical sensor up to 16,000 DPI that allows for precision and speed with a resolution accuracy of 99.4%. It also features advanced ergonomics, on board memory with cloud storage and 9 programmable buttons with durability of up to 50 million clicks.
